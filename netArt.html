Internet Artwork

For my internet artwork, I will be exploring the idea of the layers that are created on the internet and I want to use a multi-layered scratch effect to demonstrate this to the user. The layers I will be creating will be made out of collaged images that the user can scratch through as well as various links and text they will slowly be revealed as they make their way through the art piece.

I want to link my internet artwork with the idea of Relationality discussed in the Mignolo, W D. and Walsh, C E. article On Decoloniality: Concepts, Analytics, Praxis reading. This is due to how I feel the layers represent the various practices, thoughts, and ideas that we globally all have and layering them in a scratch and reveal from links to Mignolo, W D. and Walsh idea of taking pieces of each countries knowledge and incorporating them together in order to for greater world knowledge and understanding, instead of just from one particular point of view.

All material used for this project will be edited and arranged uniquely by me as I will collage the layers out of various images and resources.

The main technical concern I have is getting the multi-layered scratch patch feature to work functionally so that the user is able to endlessly scratch away and reveal layers that are also embedded with links and text. I am also a bit concerned about how I am going to fully represent the idea of Relationality in decolonization with my overall artwork.
